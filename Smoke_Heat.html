Project

Smoke & Heat Detector

About The Project

I developed a Heat and Smoke Detector to enhance fire detection systems. This critical safety device detects heat and smoke, providing early warnings to mitigate fire risks.

Utilizing 3D printing, I crafted a custom components for the detector, ensuring precision and efficiency in its design. Our proficient wiring skills seamlessly integrated electronic components for reliable detector operation.

The project highlights my ability to blend modern technology with traditional safety measures. This project represents a vital tool in safeguarding lives and property from fire hazards.
