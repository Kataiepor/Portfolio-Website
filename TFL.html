Traffic Light PCB

About The Project

Designed and implemented a fully functional traffic light circuit for Arduino in KiCad, utilizing advanced schematic and PCB layout tools to create a reliable and efficient design. The project involved integrating key components such as LEDs, resistors, and transistors, carefully arranged to ensure optimal performance and scalability for a variety of applications.

This circuit not only serves as a foundation for understanding traffic light control systems but will also be incorporated into future projects, highlighting its versatility.
